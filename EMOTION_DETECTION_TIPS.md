## 💡 Tips for Better Detection

### 1. Lighting
- ✅ Face a window or light source
- ✅ Even lighting on your face
- ❌ Avoid backlighting
- ❌ Avoid shadows on face

### 2. Camera Position
- ✅ Face the camera directly
- ✅ Keep face centered
- ✅ Stay 1-2 feet away
- ❌ Don't tilt head too much

### 3. Expression Quality
- ✅ Make **exaggerated** expressions
- ✅ Hold expression for 2-3 seconds
- ✅ One emotion at a time
- ❌ Don't make subtle expressions

### 4. Face Visibility
- ✅ Remove glasses if possible
- ✅ Hair away from face
- ✅ No hands covering face
- ✅ Clear, unobstructed view
